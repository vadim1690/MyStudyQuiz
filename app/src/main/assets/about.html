Study Quiz - digital learning

Study Quiz - digital learning lets you create quizzes of any kind. it can be used as a language learning application or for creating trivia quizzes in general.

The main purpose is to help you study in the most comfortable and efficient way, so come and be a part of the best teaching philosophy.

With this application you can create tests, add quiz questions to them and then test yourself.

Daily practice with the help of the app will help you to practice the learned material in an easy and convenient and most effective way for you.

• Creating quizzes - create quizzes according to the subject of your choice and test yourself at any time.

• Create different types of questions for the quizzes - American questions or true/false questions and test yourself in different and diverse ways.

• Get statistics on tests and answered questions and learn where your weak points are.

• A light and intuitive user interface designed to help you focus on the learning process in the best possible way.

• Ability to choose theme colors for the quizzes to customize the app specifically for you.

• Get detailed feedback after answering a quiz and know exactly where you need to improve.
